How Parents Can Help

Parents play a crucial role in helping their children navigate the online world safely. Here are some tips:

  • Discuss the risks of social media with your children.
  • Monitor your child's online activity without invading their privacy.
  • Encourage open communication about their online experiences.
  • Set boundaries and rules for social media use.
  • Be aware of the apps and platforms your child is using.
