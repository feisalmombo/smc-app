Livestreaming Safety

Livestreaming can be fun but also comes with risks. Here are some ways to stay safe while livestreaming:

  • Never share personal information during a livestream.
  • Be aware of who is watching your stream.
  • Use privacy settings to control who can see your livestream.
  • Avoid engaging with strangers during livestreams.
  • Report any suspicious or inappropriate behavior immediately.
